Bear Lake

This is a place I have spent one week of my summer almost every year. The place is just beautiful. The sand is nice, and it has fancy bathrooms for when you need to go. No large monster-sized mosquito on one side of the wall. And the night is a bit of my favorite time because we make a big bonfire and make s'mores. Dang, I want some good smore right about now.
